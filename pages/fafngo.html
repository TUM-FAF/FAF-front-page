FAF NGO

Since 2001 FAF Community has been about having a great university experience where learning goes hand in hand with making friends and life-lasting memories. In 2017 these students thought that their Community has evolved enough to go official, so on 21 of September 2017 came the moment to give this family an official name and FAF NGO has been founded as: Asociaţia Obştească Comunitatea Filierei Anglofone “Computer Science”. FAF NGO aims to strengthen the community and to continuously develop it’s members through the organized lectures, competitions, parties and other events that have the purpose to teach students soft and hard skills that will make them professionals in their future careers. FAF is a community, it is a family and moreover it is a whole experience which worths living.
